Frequently Asked Questions

What are the class schedules for 6-month courses?

Classes are held three times per week (Monday, Wednesday, Friday) with morning (9am-12pm) and evening (5:30pm-8:30pm) options available to accommodate working students.

Are there any payment plans available?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans including monthly installments and upfront payment discounts. Financial aid is also available for qualifying applicants.

What certification will I receive?

After successful completion, you'll receive a nationally recognised certificate that is accredited by relevant industry bodies and the Department of Higher Education.

Is there job placement assistance?

Yes, our career services team provides comprehensive employment support including CV guidance, interview preparation, and networking opportunities with potential employers.
