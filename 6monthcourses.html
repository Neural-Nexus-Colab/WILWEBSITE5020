Frequently Asked Questions

What are the class schedules for 6-month courses?

Classes are held three times per week (Monday, Wednesday, Friday) with morning (9am-12pm) and evening (6pm-9pm) sessions available to accommodate different schedules.

Are there any payment plans available?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans including monthly installments and upfront payment discounts. Financial assistance is also available for qualifying applicants.

What certification will I receive?

Upon successful completion, you will receive a nationally recognized certificate that is accredited by relevant bodies and the Department of Higher Education.

Is there job placement assistance?

Yes, our career services team works with local employers to help graduates find job opportunities. We offer resume building and interview preparation.
