Child Minding

30 Hours Beginner Friendly

Course Curriculum

Learn essential childcare skills including child development, safety protocols, first aid, and effective communication with children. This course covers both theoretical knowledge and practical application.

Intended Outcomes

  • Born to do around and baby needs
  • Safety measures to care and care for infants
  • Toddler needs
  • Educational toys
R750

Cooking

24 Hours All Levels

Course Curriculum

Master fundamental cooking techniques, food safety protocols, and nutritional basics. This course includes hands-on cooking sessions, ingredients selection, and budget-friendly meal preparation strategies.

Intended Outcomes

  • Nutritional requirements for a healthy body
  • Types of protein, carbohydrates and vegetables
  • Planning meals
  • How plus nutritious recipes
  • Preparation and cooking of meals
R750

Garden Maintenance

18 Hours Beginner to Intermediate

Course Curriculum

Learn practical gardening skills including soil management, plant care techniques, plant propagation techniques, and sustainable gardening practices. The course covers both classroom learning and hands-on garden work.

Intended Outcomes

  • Water restrictions and the watering requirements of indigenous and exotic plants
  • Pruning and propagation of plants
  • Planting techniques for different plant types
R750
