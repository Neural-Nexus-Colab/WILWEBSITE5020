About Empowering the Nation

Dedicated to transforming lives through accessible education and skills development since 2022

Our Mission

At Empowering the Nation, our mission is to provide quality education and practical skills training to underserved communities across South Africa. We believe that education is the most powerful tool for social transformation and economic empowerment.

Through our diverse array of courses, we aim to equip individuals with marketable skills that can help them secure employment, start their own businesses, or advance in their current careers.

Our History

Empowering the Nation was established in 2022 in Johannesburg by Precious Radebe as an initiative to address the lack of formal education and upskilling opportunities for domestic workers and gardeners.

What started as small-scale training in Precious's garage has grown into a comprehensive organization offering six-month learnerships and various short skills training programs. Starting small, the organization has grown to serve hundreds of learners, focusing on practical, hands-on courses that equip individuals ready to immediately apply in the workforce.

By 2023, it has expanded its reach within Johannesburg, incorporating more courses like web design and medical aid to make information and education more accessible.
