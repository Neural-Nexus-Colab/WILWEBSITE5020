About Empowering The Nation

Dedicated to transforming lives through accessible education and skills development since 2022

Our Mission

At Empowering the Nation, our mission is to provide quality education and practical skills training to underserved communities across South Africa. We believe that education is the most powerful tool for social transformation and economic empowerment.

Through our diverse range of courses, we aim to equip individuals with marketable skills that can help them secure employment, start their own businesses, or improve their current career prospects

Core Values

  • Innovation

Our Story

Empowering the Nation was established in 2022 in Johannesburg by Precious Radebe as an initiative to address the lack of formal education and upskilling opportunities for domestic workers and gardeners. Inspired by her parents and elderly relatives who never had the chance to advance their skills, Precious created this SME to offer both six-month learnerships and six-week short skills training programs. Starting small, the organization has grown to serve hundreds of learners, focusing on practical, hands-on courses that align with everyday needs in households and gardens.

By 2025, it has expanded its reach within Johannesburg, incorporating digital tools like a web page and mobile app to make information and registrations more accessible.
