Detailed 6-Month Professional Courses

Our comprehensive six-month programs are designed to equip you with professional skills that can transform your career prospects and enhance your personal development.

First Aid Certification

6 Months
181 Days Beginner Friendly

Become a qualified first aider with our comprehensive First Aid course. Learn basic life-saving techniques and emergency medical procedures that can make a critical difference in emergency situations.

Information Gained

  • Wounds and bleeding
  • Burns and fractures
  • Emergency scene management
  • Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR)
  • Respiratory distress e.g., Choking, blocked airway

Professional Sewing

6 Months
181 Days Beginner Friendly

Master the art of professional sewing and garment construction. This course covers everything from basic stitches to advanced garment making, preparing you for a career in fashion or textile industries.

Professional Landscaping

6 Months
181 Days Beginner Friendly

  • Fixed structures (fountains, statues, benches, tables, built-in braai)

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the class schedules for 6-month courses?

Classes are held three times per week (Monday, Wednesday, Friday) with morning and afternoon sessions available to accommodate different schedules.

Are there any payment plans available?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans including monthly installments and upfront payment discounts. Financial aid and bursaries are available for qualifying students.

What certification will I receive?

Upon successful completion, you'll receive a nationally recognized certificate that is endorsed by relevant industry bodies and the Department of Higher Education.

Is there job placement assistance?

Yes, our career services team works with local employers to help graduates find relevant employment opportunities and provides resume building and interview preparation support.
