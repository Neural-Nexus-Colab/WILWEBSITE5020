Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in any of our courses by filling out the registration form on our website or by visiting our campus during operating hours.

Can I visit the campus before enrolling?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective students to visit our campus. You can schedule a tour by contacting us through this form or calling our office.

Are there any prerequisites for the courses?

Most of our courses don't require prior knowledge, but some advanced courses may have prerequisites. Check the specific course details for more information.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 7-day cooling-off period after enrollment. Please refer to our Terms of Service for detailed information about our refund policy.

Do you offer financial aid?

Yes, we offer various financial aid options and payment plans. Contact our financial department for more details on eligibility and application process.

How can I get more information about a specific course?

You can find detailed information about each course on our website. If you need more information, feel free to contact us directly.
